About me

I'm Jonathan, a game developer and 3D artist passionate about creating games that feel alive. I've been building on Roblox since 2018, and I love the process - from thinking of a concept to seeing my creation come to life.

My work spans 3D modelling and animation, Lua scripting, Python, C#, and game systems design. I especially care about the details; whether that's the weight of an animation, the flow of a game loop, or how a world feels to explore.

When I'm not building games, I'm playing them. I take every opportunity to learn what makes games fun.
